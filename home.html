Ranked

In this game mode, you can play against other people from your or other regions from around your skill level in a competitive game. This means that your stats are tracked and your rank changes depending on this. This is a way to show your progression through the game.

Casual

In this game mode you can freestyle, relax or even socialize. It is a great place to improve with no pressure of losing ranks or other stats. This is where many freestylers go to practice mechanics. By playing some casual games before playing ranked it warms you up.

  • History of Rocket League

    Rocket League is a game released by Psyonix in July 2015. Rocket League really began in 2008 through its predecessor Supersonic Acrobatic Rocket-Powered Battle-Cars (SARPBC). It was playable on PlayStation 3. It wasn't able to gain much traction due to its complexity and its lack of marketing. But the developers realised they had something unique and made a lot of improvements and design fixes. The knew game realised quickly gained a lot of popularity. The premise of the game is, two teams of rocket-powered cars compete to score goals in a giant soccer-like arena. The high skill ceiling and high number of mechanics mean that there is a lot of improvements that a player can make. The game is always changing, finding new mechanics and better ways to play means that game is constantly evolving, players getting better and better. If you want to improve make sure to go here.
